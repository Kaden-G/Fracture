📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Control 3 of 5 Core Nodes (★ tiles) to win — or eliminate all rival factions.

🗺️ THE MAP

Nexus is a 25-tile hex grid. Node tiles are marked ★. Controlling a Node earns +1 resource per round.

🔄 TURN STRUCTURE

  • EVENT: A chaos card fires — affects ALL factions equally (AI and human)
  • INCOME: Earn 2 base + 1 per Node you control
  • 2 ACTIONS: Move, Attack, Reinforce, or Special Ability

⚔️ COMBAT

Click ATTACK → your tile → adjacent enemy tile. Both roll a d6, then add modifiers. Attacker wins ties. Loser drops 1 troop; defender at 0 → captured.

Modifiers stack on the roll:

  • 🪖 Force: +1 per 3 troops on the stack (both sides). Big armies hit harder.
  • 🛡️ Dug in: +1 per round a defender holds a tile with 2+ troops, max +3. Taking any casualty resets it. A veteran garrison can repel a larger green force.
  • ⚡ Blitz: +1 per successful attack you've already made this turn (max +2). Commit both actions to a push for momentum.

The shields on a hex show how dug-in it is. Fresh captures start at 0.

🛡️ REINFORCE

Costs 2 resources → +2 troops on your tile. Grid faction costs only 1.

🚶 MOVE

Select your tile (2+ troops) → click adjacent empty or friendly tile. Moves 1 troop.

🎭 FACTIONS — ABILITY + PERK

  • ⚙️ GRID — Industrial: Reinforce costs 1 less AND adds +3 troops (not +2)
  • 💰 SYNDICATE — Cartel: +1 resource every round · BRIBE (3 res) steals 1 troop from an adjacent enemy
  • 🌿 COMMUNE — Grassroots: +1 troop grows on a frontline tile each round · RALLY (2 res) buffs a tile + adjacent friendlies
  • 👁️ GHOST — Phantom: Your attacks ignore enemy entrenchment · SABOTAGE (2 res) removes 1 troop from any enemy tile

🔌 NODE BONUSES

Each Core Node grants its controller a passive — so which Nodes you hold matters, not just how many:

  • ⚡ POWER: Reinforce costs 1 less
  • 💧 WATER: +1 resource income per round
  • 🚇 TRANSIT: MOVE relocates 2 troops at once
  • 📡 COMMS: +1 to all your attack rolls
  • 🖧 DATA: +1 to all your defense rolls

🤝 DIPLOMACY

  • PACT: Propose a non-aggression pact to a rival — free, costs no action. Neither side may attack the other while it holds. Pacts lapse after 4 rounds.
  • BETRAYAL: Break a pact to strike and the victim gets +2 in every fight against you for 2 rounds (a grudge). The AI honors pacts too — but may betray you to seize a Node.
  • COALITION: Whoever holds 2+ Nodes is the threat — everyone else gets +1 attacking them. No runaway snowball to victory.

🃏 CHOICE EVENTS

Some events hand every faction a decision (coin vs. steel, overload vs. conserve). You pick from buttons; the AI picks for itself.

🧬 PASSIVE TRAITS

  • IRON WILL: +1 when defending
  • SWIFT STRIKE: Can move through to non-adjacent tiles
  • HOARDER: +1 bonus resource per Node
  • GHOST STEP: Move through occupied tiles
  • TACTICIAN: Roll 2 dice attack, keep highest
  • FORTIFY: Enemies need +2 to beat your defense roll
