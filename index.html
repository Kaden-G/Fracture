📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Control 3 of 5 Core Nodes (★ tiles) for 2 consecutive rounds to win — or eliminate all rival factions.

🗺️ THE MAP

Nexus is a 7×7 hex grid split into four cardinal regions around a neutral core. Node tiles are marked ★. Controlling a Node earns +1 resource per round.

🔄 TURN STRUCTURE

  • EVENT: A chaos card fires — affects ALL factions equally (AI and human)
  • INCOME: Earn 2 base + 1 per Node you control
  • 3 ACTIONS: Move, Attack, Reinforce, or Special Ability

⚔️ COMBAT

Click ATTACK → your tile → adjacent enemy tile. Both roll 2d6, then add modifiers. Attacker wins ties. Loser drops 1 troop; defender at 0 → captured.

Modifiers stack on the roll:

  • 🪖 Force: +1 per 4 troops on the stack (both sides), capped at +2. Big armies are resilient, not auto-win.
  • 🛡️ Dug in: +1 per round a defender holds a tile with 2+ troops, max +3 (or +2 on Node tiles). Taking any casualty resets it.
  • ⚔️ Press the Assault: after a winning attack you may keep striking for free — but each strike, defenders rally +2. Max 3 captures per chain. A repel, an emptied stack, or picking another action ends the assault.
  • ⚖️ Modifier Clamp: net modifier swing is capped at ±4, so no combination of perks fully removes chance.

The shields on a hex show how dug-in it is. Fresh captures start at 0.

🛡️ REINFORCE

Costs 2 resources → +2 troops on your tile. Grid faction costs only 1 resource.

💵 UNIVERSAL PAID ACTIONS (any faction)

  • ✈️ AIRLIFT (3 res): redeploy up to 3 troops between ANY two tiles you own — ignores adjacency. Buy position.
  • 🏰 ENTRENCH (2 res): add a dig-in level (+1 defense, max +3 or +2 on Nodes) to a garrisoned tile right now. Buy defense — but any casualty still resets it.

Money now buys force (Reinforce), position (Airlift), or defense (Entrench) — spend on what your plan needs.

🚶 MOVE

Select your tile (2+ troops) → click adjacent empty or friendly tile. Moves 1 troop.

🎭 FACTIONS — ABILITY + PERK

  • ⚙️ GRID — Industrial: Reinforce costs 1 less · OVERCLOCK (1 res) surges +3 troops onto a tile
  • 💰 SYNDICATE — Cartel: +1 resource every round · BRIBE (1 res) turns a troop on an adjacent enemy tile — it defects to your staging tile (−1 them, +1 you); captures the tile if it empties
  • 🌿 COMMUNE — Grassroots: +1 troop grows on a frontline tile each round · RALLY (1 res) buffs a tile + adjacent friendlies
  • 👁️ GHOST — Phantom: Your attacks ignore enemy entrenchment · SABOTAGE (1 res) hits any enemy tile for −1. The first sabotage each turn against a 2+ stack siphons that troop to your weakest frontline tile (+1)

🔌 NODE BONUSES

Each Core Node grants its controller a passive — so which Nodes you hold matters, not just how many:

  • ⚡ POWER: Reinforce costs 1 less
  • 💧 WATER: +1 resource income per round
  • 🚇 TRANSIT: AIRLIFT costs no resources
  • 📡 COMMS: +1 to all your attack rolls
  • 🖧 DATA: +1 to all your defense rolls

🤝 DIPLOMACY

  • PACT: Propose a non-aggression pact to a rival — free, costs no action. Neither side may attack the other while it holds. After 4 rounds the pact comes up for renewal — both sides choose to renew (another 4 rounds) or let it lapse, no grudge. You can even petition THE TYRANT this way — if you dare.
  • BETRAYAL: Break a pact to strike and the victim gets +2 in every fight against you for 2 rounds (a grudge). The AI honors pacts too — but may betray you to seize a Node.
  • COALITION: Whoever holds 2+ Nodes is the threat — everyone else gets +1 attacking them. No runaway snowball to victory.

🧭 REGIONS

The map is split into four cardinal regions — NORTH, EAST, SOUTH, WEST — shown as soft color-tinted zones (the center is the neutral CORE). Each tile connects to its immediate neighbors for Move/Attack. Many events strike one whole region; across a full game each region is hit equally often, in a random order — so where you commit your forces matters.

🃏 ROUND EVENTS

Every round opens with an event. Regional ones name their target region; global ones hit everyone:

  • Regional: Power Failure, The Uprising, Earthquake, Riot, Siege (−dig-in), Muster (+troops), Gold Strike (+res per tile held there).
  • Global: Market Crash (−½ res), Revolution (Node leader loses a Node), Total War (no dig-in, attackers +1), Insurgency (underdog buff).
  • Choice: Warlord's Tribute & Mercenary Contract — each faction picks coin vs. blood.

🧬 PASSIVE TRAITS

  • LAST STAND: Tiles with 1–2 troops get +3 defense — but deny Scavenger loot to attackers who break through
  • SCAVENGER: Earn +1 resource each time you capture an enemy tile
  • HOARDER: +1 bonus resource per Node
  • GHOST STEP: Move a troop up to 2 tiles, slipping through any tile (even enemy-held) to reach an empty/your tile
  • TACTICIAN: Roll 3d6 attack, keep best 2 (advantage on the bell curve)
  • FORTIFY: Fresh tiles: enemies need +2 margin. After a casualty: decays to +1
